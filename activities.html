Majoville Summer Solstice Activities/ Food

Activities

  1. Live Music by Breathe Owl Breathe
  2. Wagon Rides
  3. Flower Crown making
  4. Flower press
  5. Food Trucks: Pigs Eatin’ Ribs, Daily Blend, Good on Wheels, TC Pizza Company, and more!
  6. Games
  7. Crafts
  8. Sack Races
  9. Scavenger Hunt
  10. Bonfire with S’mores
  11. U-Pick Strawberries
  12. Children’s and pet’s costume contest
  13. Outdoor yoga
  14. Farm animal petting zoo (goats, sheep, pony, rabbits)
  15. Baseball demonstrations by the Majorville Lumberjacks
  16. Lumberjack Competitions: chopping, sawing, and speed pole climbing
  17. Blacksmithing demonstration by Dan Nickels
  18. Enjoy lemonade , lemonade slushies, ice cream, and other goodies from our local vendors. We will also have food trucks on-site for you to enjoy during our celebration.

Smores and bonfire start at dusk.
